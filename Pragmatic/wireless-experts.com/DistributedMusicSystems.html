Distributed Music Systems

The Distributed Music Systems by Pragmatic is truly a money saver for the custom A/V installer. Not only does it transmit 4 audio sources to 4 zones (expandable to 8 or 16 zones) over category 5 cables, it does so with little or no sound quality degradation at all! The Distributed Music Systems transmits their signals up to1000 feet without any issues of ground loops, noise pickups, hum, or interference. This means easier installation, better sound quality, and a significantly lower cost for the audio and video installer.
