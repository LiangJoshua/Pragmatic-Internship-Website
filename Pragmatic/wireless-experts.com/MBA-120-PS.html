High-performance, high-efficiency compact mono-block audio amplifier
The MBA-120-PS is designed for BrightSign and other similar players with Molex Mini-Fit Jr. power connectors. Using these amplifiers eliminate the need for an extra AC electrical mains outlet in digital signage installations. Most installations will only need two outlets, which are most common and standard features. (Display monitor needs one outlet and another outlet for media player and amplifier)
