PAS-8-S8M4

  • Source Components

A PA System with Active sub-woofer and 4-Channel active microphone mixer with an audio amplifier. The PAS-8-S8M4 is a high-performance ultra-compact line-array speaker featuring a wide and uniform horizontal dispersion with constant vertical beam-width and long throw distance.
