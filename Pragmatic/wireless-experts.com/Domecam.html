DomeCam™

In-Wall Security Camera System
The DomeCam™ is a high-resolution, black and white camera that transmits video through category 5 wires. With its built-in twisted pair video transmitter, the camera captures clear, detailed pictures. It is a great tool for surveillance, private security, private investigation and many other applications. DomeCam™ provides users with an option of choosing between the integrated lens and CS-Mount lens. The camera also provides mirror mode options and backlight compensation selectable on the board. This high performance 1/3" CCD camera is available in 50 or 60 Hz models.

The unit shall measure 3.5" x 3.5". Its sensor type shall be 1/3" CCD and have a sensitivity of less than 0.1 lux. Its horizontal resolution shall be greater than 380 TVL. It shall be powered by 12 V DC. It shall operate in temperatures of –25 to 55 degrees Celsius. It shall also have the option of mirror mode options and backlight compensation selectable on the board. It shall be available in 50 or 60 Hz models. It shall have a built-in twisted pair video transmitter.
