Pragmatic Communications Introduces Healthcare LCD HDTVs

Download this Press Release
Share


Santa Clara, CA, May 15, 2009 – Bringing advanced display technologies to the forefront of the healthcare industry, Pragmatic Communications will begin shipping its complete line of LCD HDTVs that enhance the overall experience for doctors and patients.

“Patient satisfaction and total cost of ownership are top priority for hospitals, and new technologies are critical to delivering superior communications, efficiency, work environment and recuperation,” said Prasanna Shah, President of Pragmatic Communications Systems, Inc.

“With features such as UL Hospital Grade certification and built-in pillow speaker ports,” he said, “Pragmatic is helping hospitals replace bulky CRTs with cable boxes and replace them with compact and stylish HDTVs that provide patients with more comforts typical of the average home and secure access to high-def programming.”

PHTV Digital Displays
Ranging in sizes from 22- to 57-inch (custom built), these UL listed hospital grade LCDs provide inroom comfort for patients, including a built-in pillow speaker port that provides the option for a pillow speaker to be connected for patient control of volume, channels and nurse call.

Key features include:

PHCLCD22: LCD Widescreen HDTV (Sizes: 22- and 26-inch)

  • UL Hospital Grade Listed
  • Digital HD PPV Compatibility
  • Built-in Pillow Speaker Port
  • HDMI input
  • Component YcbCr/YPbPr
  • S-Video + L/R Audio
  • Composite
  • VESA Compliant Wall Mountable
  • 16:9 Aspect Ratio
  • 1080P HD
  • Built-in ATSC/NTSC/QAM Clear Tuners
  • 1 Year Limited Warranty (warranty can be extended)
