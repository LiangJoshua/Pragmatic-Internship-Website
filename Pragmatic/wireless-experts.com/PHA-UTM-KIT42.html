High-performance Universal PHA Speaker TV Mounting Kit
 This kit is ideal for mounting PHA speakers to any television or video monitor with standard VESA mounting holes. Recommended for display sizes over 39" diagonal.

The kit consists of the following:
High-strength aluminum bars with matching holes with standard VESA patterns.
High-strength aluminum tube.
High-strength quick connect plastic clamps M-6 screws.
